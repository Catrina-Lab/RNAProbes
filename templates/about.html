About RNA Probes

A suite of powerful, accessible, and open-source tools for RNA detection.

Our Tools

RNA Probes provides a collection of programs for RNA probe design.

  • TFOFinder: Designs triplex-forming oligonucleotides (TFO) probes.
  • PinMol: Designs molecular beacons for live cell imaging of mRNA targets.
  • smFISH: For designing probes for single-molecule fluorescence in situ hybridization (smFISH), taking into consideration target structure.

Get Involved

RNA Probes is an open-source project, and we welcome contributions from the community. Check out our GitHub repository to learn more.
